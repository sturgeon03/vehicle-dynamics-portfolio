# Theme Tracks

## Foundation

- Chapter 1
- 이 책이 다루는 제어 문제와 시스템 지형도 정리

## Track A - Lateral Dynamics and Lane Keeping

- Chapter 2, 3
- 핵심 산출물: bicycle model 해설, lane keeping controller 분석

## Track B - Longitudinal Dynamics and Automation

- Chapter 4, 5, 6, 7

## Track C - ESC, Tire Forces, and Friction

- Chapter 8, 13, 14
- 핵심 산출물: ESC 개입 구조, tire force model, friction estimation map

## Track D - Suspension, Ride, and Rollover

- Chapter 10, 11, 12, 15

## Track E - Engine and Hybrid Control

- Chapter 9, 16
